PURPLE HAIR PRODUCTS

If you are one of many people who color your hair, you might be all too familiar with the term “brassy”: unwanted warm tones that can show up in lightened locks. While it usually occurs in dark hair that’s dyed blonde, it can also happen to hair that’s been highlighted, as sometimes the bleaching or lifting process doesn’t get rid of all the underlying pigment. And what many don’t know is that it can also be caused from things like oxidation from the sun, air exposure to minerals or even harsh shower water. The good news is that the best purple shampoos are formulated to quell the problem—often upon first wash.
